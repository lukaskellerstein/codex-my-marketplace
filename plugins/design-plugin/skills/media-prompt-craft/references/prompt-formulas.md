# Prompt Formulas

Reusable prompt templates for generating images with AI tools or finding the right stock photography. Each formula includes placeholders in [brackets], three example fills using different design aesthetics, and customization tips.

---

## Hero & Background Images

### Abstract Gradient Background

**Formula**: `Abstract [color palette] gradient background, [texture] texture, [mood] atmosphere, [lighting] lighting, suitable for [use case], high resolution`

**Example 1 (Swiss Precision)**: Abstract monochromatic gray-to-white gradient background, subtle grain texture, clean atmosphere, flat even lighting, suitable for a tech SaaS hero section, high resolution

**Example 2 (Dark Premium)**: Abstract deep navy-to-charcoal gradient background, smooth metallic texture, luxurious atmosphere, soft directional lighting, suitable for a fintech landing page, high resolution

**Example 3 (Warm Organic)**: Abstract terracotta-to-cream gradient background, paper texture, inviting atmosphere, warm golden hour lighting, suitable for a wellness brand homepage, high resolution

**Tips**: Add "seamless" if tiling. Specify aspect ratio (16:9 for heroes, 1:1 for cards).

**Formula**: `[Time of day] [landscape type], [weather/atmosphere], [color mood], shot from [perspective], [photographic style], no people`

**Example 1 (Swiss Precision)**: Overcast morning alpine lake, still water with mirror reflection, cool desaturated tones, shot from a low tripod perspective, minimalist fine art photography, no people

**Example 2 (Dark Premium)**: Twilight cityscape skyline, moody fog rolling through buildings, deep blue and amber tones, shot from rooftop perspective, cinematic wide-angle photography, no people

**Example 3 (Warm Organic)**: Golden hour rolling hills with wildflowers, soft haze in the valleys, warm earthy tones, shot from elevated hillside perspective, documentary landscape photography, no people

**Tips**: "No people" keeps it versatile for text overlay. Specify horizontal orientation explicitly if needed.

### Geometric Pattern

**Formula**: `[Shape type] geometric pattern, [color scheme], [density] arrangement, [style] aesthetic, [background color] background, seamless tileable`

**Example 1 (Swiss Precision)**: Thin-line intersecting rectangles geometric pattern, black on white, sparse arrangement, Bauhaus aesthetic, white background, seamless tileable

**Example 2 (Dark Premium)**: Hexagonal mesh geometric pattern, gold on matte black, dense arrangement, Art Deco aesthetic, black background, seamless tileable

**Example 3 (Warm Organic)**: Irregular hand-drawn circle geometric pattern, terracotta and sage, scattered arrangement, Scandinavian craft aesthetic, cream background, seamless tileable

**Tips**: "Seamless tileable" is critical for CSS backgrounds. Reduce density for better text readability on top.

### Bokeh / Particle Background

**Formula**: `[Color] bokeh lights on [background], [size variation], [density], [mood] feel, soft focus, abstract`

**Example 1 (Swiss Precision)**: White and pale gray bokeh lights on dark gray background, uniform medium size, sparse, clinical feel, soft focus, abstract

**Example 2 (Dark Premium)**: Gold and champagne bokeh lights on deep black background, mixed sizes with large dominant orbs, medium density, celebratory feel, soft focus, abstract

**Example 3 (Warm Organic)**: Soft amber and peach bokeh lights on warm brown background, gentle size variation, scattered, cozy feel, soft focus, abstract

**Tips**: Bokeh works best as a background behind translucent overlays. Specify "out of focus" to avoid sharp particles.

## Feature & Product Images

### Product Mockup

**Formula**: `[Product type] mockup, [environment/surface], [arrangement], [brand color] accents, [lighting style], clean [style] photography`

**Example 1 (Swiss Precision)**: Laptop and phone mockup, white desk surface, precise grid arrangement, no color accents, bright even studio lighting, clean minimal photography

**Example 3 (Warm Organic)**: Tablet mockup, natural wood table surface, casual lifestyle arrangement with coffee and plant, sage green accents, soft window lighting, clean lifestyle photography

## People & Lifestyle

### Professional Portrait

**Formula**: `Professional [person description], [expression], [clothing], [background setting], [lighting style], [crop] framing, [mood]`

**Example 1 (Swiss Precision)**: Professional woman in her 30s, composed neutral expression, minimal black turtleneck, plain light gray background setting, flat studio lighting, head and shoulders framing, authoritative mood

**Example 2 (Dark Premium)**: Professional man in his 40s, confident subtle smile, tailored dark suit with no tie, dark textured background setting, Rembrandt lighting, head and shoulders framing, distinguished mood

**Example 3 (Warm Organic)**: Professional young person, warm genuine smile, casual earth-toned linen shirt, outdoor garden background setting, golden hour side lighting, medium shot framing, approachable mood

**Tips**: Specify diverse representation. Avoid overly posed or stock-photo smiles by requesting "genuine" or "natural" expressions.
