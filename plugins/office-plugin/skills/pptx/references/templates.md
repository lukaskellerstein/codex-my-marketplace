# Design Templates

Select a template based on the presentation's purpose. Each template provides a complete, pre-tested design system: colors, fonts, motif, slide rhythm, and background strategy. **Always select a template BEFORE choosing colors and fonts individually.**

---

## Pitch Deck

For startup pitches, investor presentations, product launches.

- **Palette**: Dark base (`0F172A`) + bright accent (`38BDF8` or `F97316`)
- **Fonts**: Arial Black / Calibri
- **Motif**: Color-block headers (solid-color band at top of content slides)
- **Rhythm**: Dark title → Light problem → Dark solution → Light metrics → Dark CTA
- **Backgrounds**: 3+ photo-backed slides, bold solid colors between
- **Key features**: Large stat callouts (72pt+), one idea per slide, minimal bullets, dramatic size contrast
- **Stat style**: Bold Stats layout on colored backgrounds, not white cards
- **Energy**: High

---

## Corporate Quarterly

For quarterly reviews, board presentations, strategy updates.

- **Palette**: Slate & Gold (`2C3E50` / `BDC3C7` / `F39C12`) or Nordic Blue
- **Fonts**: Georgia / Calibri Light
- **Motif**: Side accent strip (0.15" vertical bar on left edge of content slides)
- **Rhythm**: Title → Agenda → Content (3-5 slides) → Data (2 slides) → Summary
- **Backgrounds**: Photo on title and close only, clean whites/grays for content
- **Key features**: Native charts, clean data tables, restrained decoration
- **Stat style**: White cards on light gray — classic and trustworthy
- **Energy**: Low-medium

---

## Tech / Product

For product demos, technical overviews, SaaS presentations, AI topics.

- **Palette**: Electric Violet (`0F0A1A` / `7C3AED` / `DDD6FE`) or Neon Pulse (`0D0D0D` / `1A1A2E` / `00F5D4`)
- **Fonts**: Calibri / Calibri Light (or Century Gothic / Calibri)
- **Motif**: Rounded image frames (all images with 0.12-0.15" radius)
- **Rhythm**: Dark title → Screenshot/demo → Features (dark cards) → Architecture → Closing
- **Backgrounds**: Dark theme throughout, accent glows via subtle OVALs
- **Key features**: Dark elevated cards, icon rows, code-adjacent fonts for technical terms
- **Stat style**: Dark cards with bright accent numbers
- **Energy**: Medium-high

---

## Educational / Workshop

For training sessions, workshops, educational lectures, onboarding.

- **Palette**: Transformative Teal (`1B2838` / `0D9488` / `5EEAD4`) or Forest & Moss
- **Fonts**: Trebuchet MS / Calibri
- **Motif**: Corner badge with section number (small rounded rect in top-right)
- **Rhythm**: Title → Agenda → Content alternating with exercise/activity slides → Summary
- **Backgrounds**: Light base, colorful section dividers, minimal photos
- **Key features**: High contrast for readability, clear numbering, 16pt body minimum, generous spacing
- **Stat style**: Simple, clear — avoid flashy cards
- **Energy**: Medium

---

## Creative / Portfolio

For design portfolios, creative pitches, brand presentations, photography showcases.

- **Palette**: Berry & Cream (`6D2E46` / `A26769` / `ECE2D0`) or Sunset Gradient
- **Fonts**: Palatino / Garamond
- **Motif**: Generous whitespace (no decorative elements at all)
- **Rhythm**: Full-bleed photo → Minimal text → Photo → Text → Photo close
- **Backgrounds**: 60%+ slides with photo backgrounds
- **Key features**: Photography-first, minimal text (max 20 words/slide), editorial feel, asymmetric text placement
- **Stat style**: Photo Hero with bottom text overlay
- **Energy**: Variable (matches the imagery)

---

## Minimalist Executive

For executive summaries, C-suite briefs, high-stakes strategic decisions.

- **Palette**: Charcoal Minimal (`36454F` / `F2F2F2` / `212121`) or Soft Dreamscape (`F8F4F0` / `E8D5C4` / `C4A882`)
- **Fonts**: Segoe UI / Segoe UI Light (or Calibri / Calibri Light)
- **Motif**: Generous whitespace (no decorative elements)
- **Rhythm**: Title → One key point per slide → Data → Close
- **Backgrounds**: Clean solid colors only, no photos
- **Key features**: Maximum 15 words per slide, 36-48pt body text, no bullets ever, one idea = one slide
- **Stat style**: Single stat per slide at 72-96pt, centered
- **Energy**: Low (restrained, confident)

---

## Bold Marketing

For product launches, campaign presentations, event pitches, sales decks.

- **Palette**: Coral Energy (`F96167` / `F9E795` / `2F3C7E`) or Cherry Bold (`990011` / `FCF6F5` / `2F3C7E`)
- **Fonts**: Impact / Arial (or Arial Black / Calibri)
- **Motif**: Color-block headers with full-width color bands
- **Rhythm**: Splash title → Problem → Solution → Social proof → Features → CTA
- **Backgrounds**: High-saturation solid colors alternating with full-bleed photos
- **Key features**: Oversized headlines (48-60pt), vibrant color blocking, high-energy feel, charSpacing on uppercase headers
- **Stat style**: Bold Stats on saturated background colors
- **Energy**: High

## How to Use Templates

1. **Read the topic** — what is the presentation about?
2. **Match to a template** — pick the one whose purpose and energy level fits best
3. **Apply the entire system** — use the template's palette, fonts, motif, rhythm, and background strategy as a coherent whole
4. **Customize from there** — you can swap specific colors or fonts, but maintain the template's overall design philosophy
5. **Don't mix templates** — if you start with Pitch Deck, don't add Minimalist Executive's whitespace approach mid-deck. Commit to one visual language.
